Kinetic Atlas Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-25

Kinetic Atlas is published by PierDo. This policy describes the current build.

Data Stored On Your Device

Kinetic Atlas stores training state on your device, including baseline progress, current atlas rung, completed session summaries, free CV session count, and local entitlement state. This data is used to show your guided next session and progress feedback.

You can delete this local data by clearing app storage in Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

Camera Use

Kinetic Atlas uses your device camera for local pose detection, rep counting, and form feedback during training sessions. Camera frames are processed on your device. The current build does not upload, record, or store camera video or images.

Camera access is optional for the rest of the app. If camera permission is blocked, simulated verification controls remain available in the current build.

Purchases

Kinetic Atlas Pro is designed to use Google Play Billing and RevenueCat for subscription purchases and entitlement status. When enabled, Google Play and RevenueCat may process purchase identifiers, product identifiers, receipts, and subscription status so the app can unlock Pro features.

The app does not store payment-card details. Payment details are handled by Google Play.

Accounts, Ads, And Analytics

Kinetic Atlas does not require an account in this build. It does not include advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs in this milestone.

Third-Party Services

  • MediaPipe Tasks Vision for on-device pose detection.
  • RevenueCat Purchases for future subscription entitlement checks.
  • Google Play Billing through RevenueCat when subscriptions are configured.
